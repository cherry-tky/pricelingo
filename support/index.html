Support

How do I convert a price?

Select a complete price such as $49.99, 29 EUR, or £1,299. Click the small green conversion button beside the selection.

How do I change my preferred currency?

Click the PriceLingo toolbar icon and choose a currency under Convert prices to.

Why was a symbol interpreted incorrectly?

Plain $, ¥, and kr symbols can refer to multiple currencies. Open Currency symbol settings from the toolbar popup to choose your preferred interpretation.

Why might a conversion differ from my bank?

PriceLingo uses informational daily reference rates. Banks, card issuers, and payment services may use live rates and add fees or margins.

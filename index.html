Questions

About the PriceLingo currency converter add-on

Which browsers does PriceLingo support?

PriceLingo is a Manifest V3 extension built for Chromium browsers, including Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge.

Are the exchange rates live trading rates?

No. PriceLingo uses informational daily reference rates, so banks and payment services may show different totals.

Does PriceLingo track browsing?

No. PriceLingo has no ads, analytics, or accounts. Read the privacy policy for full details.
